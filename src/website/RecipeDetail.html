Veggie Pasta

Comforting and quick. Static demo page.

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. Sauté vegetables with olive oil.
  3. Combine pasta, veggies, and sauce.
  4. Top with herbs and serve warm.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic, salt, pepper
